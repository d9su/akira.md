About us

At Akira, we believe in using technology to change people's lives. We're passionate people who want to make health care better.

On-demand medical advice

Our staff physicians have your medical records and can provide immediate, personalized advice via secure text. If they need to see you, a quick video call can be initiated right from the app or in a browser. We see every interaction as an opportunity to learn more about you so we can provide better care in the future.

What Akira doctors can do

90% of medical concerns can be addressed using Akira

Answer questions about your health via secure text or video
Diagnose and treat medical issues without a physical examination
Work with your family doctor for more complex issues
Write prescriptions and send them to your pharmacy
Refer to your specialists or allied health professionals
Order tests and imaging instantly through your phone

Your personal health assistant

Personal Health Assistants are the missing link in health management

Your Personal Health Assistant’s job is to guarantee that you and your loved ones live a healthy life. They will work with you to create a long-term strategy for your health, taking into account any chronic conditions, dietary concerns, and lifestyle goals.

Your Personal Health Assistant is a health advocate for you and your family. They will help you schedule specialist appointments, coordinate care for elderly parents or children, and even book medical travel.
