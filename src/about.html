No More Waiting Rooms

Akira is designed to work from anywhere, just like our members. Save a visit to the doctor in 90% of cases - and say goodbye to waiting rooms.

What Akira Doctors Can Do

Our doctors are friendly, knowledgeable, and experienced.

Answer questions about your health via secure text or video
Diagnose and treat many common medical issues
Write prescriptions and send them to your preferred pharmacy
Refer you to specialists or allied health professionals
Order tests and send them to a lab near you
Work with your family doctor to manage chronic conditions

Your Personal Health Assistant (PHA)

Personal Health Assistants are the missing link in preventive care

Every Akira member is assigned a PHA. They will work with you to create a long-term strategy for your health, taking into account any chronic conditions, dietary concerns, and lifestyle goals.

Your PHA will also schedule specialist appointments, coordinate care for elderly parents or children, and even book medical travel.
