About us

We're building a world-class medical experience that is radically more convenient than anything available today. We're on a mission to bring Canadian healthcare into the 21st century - and beyond.

No More Waiting Rooms

Our staff physicians have your medical records and can provide immediate, personalized advice via secure text. If they need to see you, a quick video call can be initiated right from the app or in a browser. We see every interaction as an opportunity to learn more about you so we can provide better care in the future.

What Akira Doctors Can Do

We can save you a trip to the doctor's office 90% of the time.

Answer questions about your health via secure text or video
Diagnose and treat many common medical issues
Write prescriptions and send them to your preferred pharmacy
Refer you to specialists or allied health professionals
Order tests and send them to a lab near you
Work with your family doctor to manage chronic conditions

Your Personal Health Assistant (PHA)

Personal Health Assistants are the missing link in preventive care

Every Akira member is assigned a PHA, accessible through the Akira app. They will work with you to create a long-term strategy for your health, taking into account any chronic conditions, dietary concerns, and lifestyle goals.

Your PHA will also schedule specialist appointments, coordinate care for elderly parents or children, and even book medical travel. For whatever health-related needs you might have, your PHA is ready to help.

A Complete Mobile Health Picture

Read the notes your doctor takes during your discussions
See your doctor’s treatment plan and recommendations
